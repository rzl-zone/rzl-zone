## About the Code of Conduct [#code-of-conduct]

The **Code of Conduct** represents our collective commitment to fostering a **respectful**, **inclusive**, and **supportive** community around `@rzl-zone/utils-js`.  
It outlines the expectations for how we interact with one another and provides guidance for addressing behavior that violates these principles.

By participating in this project — through code contributions, documentation, discussions, or community spaces — you agree to uphold these standards and help maintain an environment where everyone feels **safe, valued, and empowered to contribute**.

<SeparatorSection />

## Our Standards

### ✅ Positive and supportive behavior includes: [!toc]
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others.  
- Being respectful, inclusive, and considerate in all interactions.  
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.  
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback graciously.  
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community as a whole.  

### 🚫 Unacceptable behavior includes: [!toc]
- Harassment, personal attacks, or derogatory remarks.  
- Discriminatory jokes or language.  
- Public or private harassment of any kind.  
- Publishing others’ private information without consent.  
- Any behavior that would be inappropriate in a professional or community environment.  

<SeparatorSection />

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.  
They have the right to take appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior deemed unacceptable — including issuing warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the community.

<SeparatorSection />

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — including code repositories, discussions, issue trackers, social media, and any public interactions where someone represents the project or its community.

<SeparatorSection />

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness any form of harassment, abuse, or other unacceptable behavior, please report it through one of the following channels:

- 📧 **Email:** [rzlzone.dev@gmail.com](mailto:rzlzone.dev@gmail.com).  
- 🔗 **GitHub Issues:** [UtilsJS Repository Issues](https://github.com/rzl-zone/utils-js/issues).

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.  
Project maintainers will determine the appropriate response based on the situation, prioritizing the safety and well-being of the community.
